Tutorials & Lessons
Progress from posture to pro speed with focused micro-lessons.
Lesson 01
Home Row Foundation
Anchor your fingers on ASDF and JKL; — every key is just a small movement away.
  • Index on F and J (feel the bumps)
  • Eyes off the keyboard
  • Rest wrists, float fingers
Lesson 02
Top & Bottom Rows
Reach up to QWERTY and down to ZXCVB without losing home position.
  • Return to home after every reach
  • Practice common bigrams: th, in, er
  • Use the correct finger every time
Lesson 03
Numbers & Symbols
Train the number row and shifted symbols last — they're slow for everyone.
  • Glance, don't stare
  • Use right Shift for left-hand symbols
  • Drill phone numbers and dates
Lesson 04
Rhythm Over Speed
A steady beat beats bursty typing. Aim for even key intervals.
  • Type to a metronome at 80 bpm
  • Smooth, not fast
  • Slow down through hard words
Lesson 05
Accuracy First
Speed without accuracy is wasted motion. Push for 97%+ before chasing wpm.
  • Backspace is a tax
  • Pause, don't panic
  • Re-read the word before typing
Lesson 06
Real-World Drills
Practice on text you'll actually type: emails, code, essays.
  • Copy your own writing
  • Type a paragraph from a book
  • Practice 10 minutes daily

Pro Tips for Real Speed
The compounding habits that separate 40 wpm from 100+.
🧘
Posture matters
Feet flat, back straight, screen at eye level. Tension in shoulders kills speed.
👀
Never look down
Cover your hands with a cloth for a week. Painful at first, transformative after.
⚡
Warm up
Two minutes of slow accurate typing before any test. Cold fingers miss keys.
🎯
Accuracy > speed
Speed is a side-effect of accuracy. Optimize the right metric.
🛑
Take breaks
Micro-breaks every 20 minutes. Your tendons will thank you in ten years.
📈
Track progress
Log your best WPM weekly. Small wins compound into massive gains.
